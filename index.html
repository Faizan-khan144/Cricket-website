Cricket is a bat-and-ball game that began in England in the 16th century.

It is now popular all over the world, especially in countries like Pakistan, India, Australia, and England.

The game has evolved over the years, with different formats like Test, One Day International (ODI), and Twenty20 (T20).

Cricket is governed by the International Cricket Council (ICC), which organizes major tournaments like the Cricket World Cup.

Cricket has a rich history, with legendary players like Sir Donald Bradman, Sachin Tendulkar, and Brian Lara.

The game is known for its spirit of sportsmanship and fair play, often referred to as "the gentleman's game".

Cricket has a unique culture, with traditions like the tea break and the singing of national anthems before matches.
